Lasagna

Description

Best Lasagna Recipe Lasagna is a classic Italian dish featuring layers of wide, flat pasta, typically combined with ragù (a meat sauce), béchamel (a white sauce), and various cheeses, then baked until bubbly and golden brown.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Meat
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Spinach
  • Mozzarella
  • Garlic
  • Lasagna Pasta

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Cook meat with garlic, drain well and add pasta sauce. Simmmer for a few minutes
  3. Combine cheese mixture in a bowl
  4. Layer the meat sauce adn cheese with lasanga pasta and bake until the top of the lasgna is golden brown
